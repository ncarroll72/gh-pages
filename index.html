Claris Clusters

A powerful, purpose-built WordPress plugin for law firm websites.

Designed specifically for law firms, Claris Clusters combines robust SEO with seamless content management. Its unique clustering structure—Firm, Team, and Practice Areas—optimizes site architecture, internal linking, and load times for maximum search engine visibility.

  • SEO-Optimized Site Structure: Enhance visibility with optimized internal linking and fast load times.
  • Cascading Clusters: Gain insights into your content distribution with an intuitive admin area that tracks content across clusters.
  • Efficient Content Management: Simplify updates and organization with a system tailored for legal content.
  • Future Enhancements: Roadmap includes features like lawyer blogging, integrated analytics, and smart internal linking.
